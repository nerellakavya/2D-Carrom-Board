The code is basically implementation of carrom board game.

Keyboard Controls:

--> Right Arrow to move the striker on the line of shooting towards right.
--> Left Arrow to move the striker on the line of shooting towards left.
-->Up Arrow increases the speed upto a defined maximum value.
-->Down Arrow decreases the speed till a defined threshold value
-->Pressing "a" changes the direction of shooting anticlockwise.
-->Pressing "c" changes the direction of shooting clockwise.

Scoring:

-->The game begins with a score of 30.Each second costs a point.
-->Each white coin pocketed adds a score of 10 while each black coin pocketed costs the player a score of 5. 
(Assuming that the player plays for whites.)
-->Striker pocketes does not cost score (Again, a design choice).
